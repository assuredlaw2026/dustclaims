About Assured Law

Assured Law is a Henderson, Nevada law firm focused on practical, results-driven representation. The firm pursues private enforcement actions on behalf of homeowners affected by construction dust, air quality violations, and permit non-compliance.

DustClaims.com was created to give homeowners a clear path to enforcement when government agencies fail to act. When you file a complaint and nothing happens, when the dust keeps coming, when your pool is contaminated and your HVAC filters are clogged, you have legal options. Assured Law exists to pursue them.
